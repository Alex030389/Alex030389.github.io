Procedure for Guarantee claim

To claim the Guarantee, you must submit the Examination Score Report in PDF format within 7 days of Test to billing@test-inside.com. We will then issue Refund to the same card that is used to make payment.

Guarantee Claim is Un VALID in the following cases:

  1. Retired or Wrong Exam purchase Test-Inside.com will not entertain any claims once the product is Downloaded and Installed.
  2. Guarantee cant be claimed on any exam material that was provided for Free or any exam purchased on discount.
  3. Expired order that are no longer accessible or outdated exam products for which update is already released.
  4. TestInside provides only practice questions for Avaya, CISSP, Cisco, EMC, HP, Microsoft, Oracle, PMI and SSCP exams, these are not covered by 100% pass and refund Guarantee.
